Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

1. What age do I need to be to start driving lessons?

Students can start taking driving lessons at the age of 16.

2. Do I need to have a learner's permit before taking lessons?

Yes, you must have a learner's permit to take driving lessons.

3. How many lessons will I need to pass the driving test?

The number of lessons varies for each student, but on average, 10 to 15 lessons are recommended.

4. What if I miss a scheduled lesson?

If you miss a lesson, please contact us as soon as possible to reschedule. Our cancellation policy allows for rescheduling with at least 24 hours' notice.

5. Are your instructors certified?

Yes, all our instructors are fully certified and have extensive teaching experience.

6. What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash, credit cards, and online payments through our website.
